## MOSFET Visualizer

An interactive tool for visualizing MOSFET drain current characteristics. Supports NMOS/PMOS devices in both enhancement and depletion modes. Drag the graph to adjust voltages in real-time.

<div class="demo-container">
  <p class="demo-link"><a href="/assets/demos/mosfet-visualizer/index.html" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Open MOSFET Visualizer (new tab)</a></p>
</div>

**Features:**
- Toggle between NMOS and PMOS device types
- Switch between Enhancement and Depletion operating modes
- Adjust terminal voltages (V<sub>G</sub>, V<sub>D</sub>, V<sub>S</sub>) and device parameters (V<sub>th</sub>, k)
- Interactive I-V characteristic plot with drag-to-adjust
- Real-time computation of V<sub>GS</sub>, V<sub>DS</sub>, I<sub>D</sub>, operating region, and overdrive voltage
- Draggable card-based UI
